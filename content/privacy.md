When you book a ride or contact us, we may collect:

- Your name
- Phone number

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others. We may share information:

- With service providers who assist our business operations

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, use, or disclosure. However, no internet transmission is completely secure.

## Your Rights

Under Australian privacy law, you have the right to:

- Access your personal information

- Opt out of marketing communications

## Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
